Why Organ Donation Matters

Thousands of people are waiting for organ transplants. Your decision to become an organ donor can change lives. Organs that can be donated include the heart, kidneys, liver, lungs, pancreas, and intestines. Tissues like corneas ,skin, bone, and heart valves can also be donated.

Anyone can be a potential donor, regardless of age, race, or medical history.
